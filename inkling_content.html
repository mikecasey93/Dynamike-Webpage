My Inkling Bio:

I picked up Inkling as my one rushdown based fighter as all my others are primarily zoners. I mainly rely on Inkling in matchups against fast fighters and opponents who are difficult to zone. I'm a huge fan of Inkling's aerials and neutral B which is a useful zoning tool of its own. I do however struggle with Inkling against swordies as disjoints I find can give Inkling trouble. Any pro tips would be greatly appreciated via Discord.
